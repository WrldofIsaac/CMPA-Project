# TrackWave

# What is this
TrackWave is a music platform that is an alternative to Multitracks. The catcher is, it is designed for all genres, instead of mainly worship music.
It's a website where users can explore, upload, and access multitrack audio for different types of music like rap, r&b and many more. The goal is to 
make this feel real and functional, so producers oridinary people or even musicians will find this super helpful and high-quality. 

# Why does it exist?
I created TrackWave because platforms like multitracks are very niche when it comes to music and don't really suppose a wide ranges of genres.
That makes it harder for people outside of the worship space to find what they need. So TrackWave solves that problem, by introducing many different genres. It's meant to open up opportunities for creativity and collaboration across all styles of music.
